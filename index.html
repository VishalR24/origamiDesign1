Learning About the Scorpion

  1. Scorpions have eight legs just like the spider and they have a venomous tail with a stinger at the end of it.
  2. Scorpions do not have bones, instead they have an exoskeleton made of chitin, which is similar to the shell of a shrimp.
  3. Scorpions can survive just about anything such as harsh weather conditions and more.
